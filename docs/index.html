Fast Variational Method for the Estimation and Quantization
of non-flat Displacements
in 1D Signals with Applications in Neuroimaging

Philipp Flotho1, 2, David Thinnes1, 3, Jonas F. Vibell3, Daniel J. Strauss1

1Systems Neuroscience & Neurotechnology Unit, Faculty of Medicine, Saarland University, Germany
2Summer Program, Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS), Tokyo, Japan
3Department of Psychology, University of Hawai'i at Mānoa .

Summary

This work uses principles from variational optical flow estimation and applies those to the alignment of 1D data.
